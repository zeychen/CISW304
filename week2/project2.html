Find Your Zodiac Animal

Instructions

Hover on each of the 12 boxes and find your birth year and then click on your birth year box to find your Zodiac animal.

If your birth year is not shown, please add/subtract multiples of 12 to your birth year until it is within range of the years shown. Ex: if your birth year is 1964, then adding 24 (a multiple of 12) to 1964 gives 1988 which falls within the range of years given.
